Motion + Sound. Receipts in motion.

A sandbox for the brand's motion language and sonic palette. Every animation lives within the existing motion tokens (120 / 200 / 320 ms). Every sound is synthesized on the fly with Web Audio — no asset files, so we can tune them live and ship only the ones that earn it. Once you mark favorites, we'll port them into the main brand guideline.

— Sonic palette · synth cues
cue.detect

Scam detected.

Sharp downward sweep + a brief noise burst. Use the second a verdict crosses below 40. ~280ms total.

cue.safe

Verdict · safe.

Two-note rise (C5 → E5) on a soft sine. Confirms a "you're good" moment. ~220ms.

cue.tap

Micro-tap.

Tiny sine click at 1.2 kHz with a 40 ms decay. Optional on primary CTA press — quieter than a system tick.

cue.tick

Counter bump.

Soft mid-pop. Fires when the peer-report counter increments. Doesn't compete with speech.

cue.scan

Scan loop.

Filtered pink-noise sweep, looped. Used during the 800 ms while a domain is being checked. Auto-fades on result.

cue.block

Blocked.

Deflected "thunk" + low-pass noise tail. Plays when the user taps "Back to safety" on a danger state.
