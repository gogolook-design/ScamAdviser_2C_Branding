# ScamAdviser B2C — Logo & Lockup

The mark is `bs/d` — the slash cuts through the word the way we cut through the noise. Source: `scamadviser-b2c-brand.html` §02.

## Mark

- **Symbol:** `bs/d`
- **Slash:** always skewed 10deg — this is the signal.
- **Wordmark:** ScamAdviser logotype + "digital bs detector" descriptor.

## Contexts (8 lockups)

| Context | Background | Mark Color | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary light | Anti-flash / Warm Grey | Ink Black + Red slash | Default — most surfaces |
| Alert red | SA Red `#CC1417` | White | Danger + warnings only |
| Reverse dark | Ink Black / Dark UI | White | Editorial, video chrome |
| Wordmark full | Paper | Full logotype + descriptor | Press, footer |
| Favicon | Red square `#CC1417` | White slash | App icon, tab favicon (16–1024px) |

## Clear Space

- Minimum clear space = cap-height of the slash ("x").
- Nothing crops into that frame — not type, not photography, not texture.

## Minimum Sizes

| Medium | Minimum |
|---|---|
| Digital | 24px mark height |
| Print | 8mm mark height |

## Never

- Gradients on the mark — ever.
- Rotation or skew beyond the built-in 10deg of the slash.
- Red slash on a red background.
- Shadow, outline, or container around the mark.
- Reconstruct or distort the mark.
